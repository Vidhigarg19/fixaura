See How FixAura Works in 5 Simple Steps

From pointing your camera to finishing your repair — here's exactly what happens, step by step.

1
Step 1 of 5
1

Point Your Camera at the Broken Item

Open FixAura and point your phone at anything broken — a leaking tap, loose switch, broken furniture, or dirty fan.

💡 Tip: Good lighting = faster detection

2

AI Instantly Identifies the Problem

FixAura's AI analyzes the object in milliseconds, detects the exact issue, and tells you what's broken.

💡 Confidence above 90% = proceed confidently

3

Follow Animated AR Arrows & Highlight Rings

Glowing teal arrows point exactly where to turn, tighten, or replace. Highlight rings show which parts to touch.

💡 Use voice commands to keep hands free

4

Risky Steps? Safety Bubbles Protect You

For dangerous steps, FixAura shows a glowing amber confirmation prompt. It ensures you turn off power or water before proceeding.

⚠️ Never skip a safety bubble

5

Repair Complete — Or Connect to a Human Expert

When you finish, you'll see a celebration screen. If the AI isn't sure, FixAura seamlessly connects you to a human repair expert.

🎉 First 3 repairs are free
